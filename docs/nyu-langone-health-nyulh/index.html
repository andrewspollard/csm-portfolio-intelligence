Use Case / Pain / Solve / Outcomes

SFDC Chorus
Pain

19,000-employee academic medical center with massive nursing/clinical hiring volume. Manual recruiting workflows, fragmented onboarding across dozens of departments, compliance complexity in healthcare hiring (I-9, credentialing, background checks at scale).

Solve

Rival Recruit handles high-volume nursing/clinical TA workflows including job posting, applicant tracking, and candidate communication. Rival Onboarding manages day-one compliance and credentialing paperwork at scale across multiple NYU entities and geographies.
