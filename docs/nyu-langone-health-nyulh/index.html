Use Case / Pain / Solve / Outcomes

SFDC Chorus

The Pain

19,000-employee academic medical center with massive nursing/clinical hiring volume. Manual recruiting workflows that couldn't scale. Fragmented onboarding across dozens of departments. Compliance complexity in healthcare hiring (I-9, credentialing, background checks). Each NYU entity was handling hiring differently with no unified process.

What Rival Solves

Rival Recruit handles high-volume nursing/clinical TA workflows across all NYU entities. Rival Onboarding manages day-one compliance and credentialing paperwork at scale. Unified candidate experience across all departments. Self-service portal for candidates reduces HR administrative load.

Outcomes Delivered

Centralized TA workflow for nursing pipeline (heaviest volume). Streamlined onboarding compliance across multiple entities. Self-service candidate experience deployed (help center, localization, automated notifications). Active expansion: 20 additional R/RM licenses on latest renewal to extend into academic departments. 15.5-year retention demonstrates deep embedding.
