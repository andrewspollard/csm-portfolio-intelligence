Crane Pumps & Systems (CP&S) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Crane Holdings Co, a diversified industrial conglomerate listed on the NYSE. CP&S designs and manufactures high-performance centrifugal pumps for municipal, commercial, industrial, and wastewater applications. Brands include Barnes, Burks, Crown, Deming, Prosser, and Weinman. All manufacturing is domestic — the Piqua, Ohio facility has been the production hub since 1965.

In 2026, CP&S launched the Atlas Series (Barnes sewage ejector/effluent pumps, shipping July 2026) and the SyFlo Series (2025, high-efficiency solids handling submersibles). The company has been posting record hiring volumes across Sales, IT, Engineering, and Operations in early 2026 — the highest in 12 months per ZoomInfo hiring signals.

CP&S is a lean manufacturing organization. HR team is small (~3-5 FTEs) but active: VP HR Katy Zimpfer leads, supported by HR Manager Hailey Mescher and two generalists.
