Company Overview
Who They Are
MarketAxess Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: MKTX) is the leading operator of an electronic fixed-income trading platform serving institutional investors and broker-dealers worldwide. Founded in 2000 and headquartered in New York, MarketAxess enables trading in U.S. corporate bonds, emerging market bonds, high-yield, Eurobonds, munis, CDS, and ETFs via proprietary protocols including Open Trading, Portfolio Trading (PT), and Mid-X.

The company is publicly traded, has 859 employees across 7 global locations, and generated $871M in revenue in FY2025. Q1 2026 delivered record revenue of $233.4M (+12% YoY). Key differentiators include its CP+ AI pricing engine, Adaptive Algo automation platform, and Trax post-trade services division.
