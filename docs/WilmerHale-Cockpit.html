Executive Summary

WilmerHale uses Rival for two core HR workflows: Onboarding (Rival Workflow) and Recruiting (Rival Recruit), plus an "Other" category covering integrations and platform add-ons. The firm's annual hiring volume includes partner elevations (14/year), lateral attorney hires (above-market trend in 2026), and staff recruitment across 19 offices and 2,350 employees. Rival handles the complexity that standard HRIS platforms cannot: multi-step, conditional onboarding flows with practice group-specific variations, compliance tasks (I-9/E-Verify), and a full ATS pipeline for external and internal candidates. The platform has been in use for ~15 years. Active cases show day-to-day operational reliance for task reopening, form management, and integration stability. Key contacts are HRIS-level administrators — CHRO relationship is not yet established.
